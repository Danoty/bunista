Bunista Sacco Society Ltd was registered in November 2010 under the Co-operative Society’s Act Cap 490, Registration Number 12880. The Sacco draws its membership from Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University employees and their spouses and continues to deliver steady financial growth, convenience, and member-focused service.
