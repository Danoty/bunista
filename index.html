Bunista Sacco Society Ltd was registered in November 2010 under the Co-operative Society’s Act Cap 490, Registration Number 12880. The Sacco serves employees of Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University and their spouses and continues to deliver steady financial growth, convenience, and member-focused service.
