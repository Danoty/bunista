Bunista Sacco Society Ltd was registered in November 2010 under the Co-operative Society’s Act Cap 490, Registration Number 12880. The Sacco draws its membership from Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University of Science and Technology employees and their spouses.

Welcome

Bunista has achieved steady growth since inception. The Sacco continues to strengthen its social and economic base through savings, share capital, non-withdrawable deposits and convenient credit products for members.

Its main objective is to organize and promote the welfare and economic interest of members within a trusted co-operative framework.

What Bunista stands for

Built on sound co-operative values and practical member-centered financial support.

Encourage thrift

Support disciplined saving among members for long-term personal and family growth.

Promote dignity

Offer products and services that strengthen economic and social wellbeing.

Provide education

Educate members on proper use of credit, poverty eradication and co-operation.
