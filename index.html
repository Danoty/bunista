Trusted savings, smart credit, and a polished digital home for Bunista members.

Bunista Sacco Society Ltd was registered in November 2010 under the Co-operative Societies Act Cap 490, Registration Number 12880. The Sacco draws its membership from employees of Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University and their spouses, and has continued to grow steadily in membership, share capital, and non-withdrawable deposits.
