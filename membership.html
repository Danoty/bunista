Membership

Bunista membership is designed for individuals within the Sacco’s common bond and offers a structured path into savings, participation, and access to credit and member services.

EligibilityEmployee of JOOUST or spouse of JOOUST staff.
ApplicationApply using the prescribed membership forms.
Entrance FeeMembership fee of Kshs. 1,000.00.
👥

Who can join

One must be within the common bond of Bunista, namely an employee of JOOUST or a spouse of JOOUST staff.

📝

How to join

Prospective members are required to complete the prescribed forms and submit them through the official application process.

🌱

Why join

Membership opens the door to structured savings, cooperative participation, relevant financial products, and long-term personal growth.

Membership Duties

What members are expected to do

✓
Attend and participate

Take part in Annual General Meetings and Member Education Days.

✓
Offer leadership

Members may be elected to organs of the society and help shape its direction.

✓
Use the services responsibly

Enjoy the facilities and services of the Sacco with accountability and good standing.

✓
Comply with the framework

Observe policies, by-laws, Cap 490, rules, and AGM resolutions.

Member experience

Membership in Bunista is more than account ownership. It is participation in a cooperative community built around savings discipline, shared opportunity, and responsible economic support.
