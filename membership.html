Membership

Bunista membership is open to those within the common bond. Membership pages have been organized clearly to make eligibility, duties, and application requirements easy to understand.

Membership Eligibility

  • One must be within the common bond, that is, an employee of JOOUST.
  • Spouse of JOOUST staff.
  • Apply using the prescribed forms.
  • Pay an entrance fee of Kshs. 1,000.00.

Membership Application

Prospective members should complete the prescribed membership forms and submit the required details for processing in line with Bunista procedures.

Variation Applications

Members may also use the required forms to make approved variations where applicable under Bunista guidelines.

Duties of Members

  • Attend and participate in decision making at Annual General Meetings and Member Education Days.
  • Be elected to organs of the society.
  • Enjoy the use of facilities and services of the society.
  • Comply with policies, By-Laws, the Co-operative Society’s Act Cap 490, rules and AGM resolutions.

Membership support

For guidance on joining, updating details, or general membership support, contact Bunista directly through the official office line or WhatsApp button available on every page of this site.
