Membership Eligibility and Duties

Membership is built around the common bond of Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University employees and their spouses, guided by Bunista by-laws and co-operative values.

Membership eligibility

  • One must be within the common bond, that is, an employee of JOOUST.
  • Spouse of JOOUST staff.
  • Apply using the prescribed forms.
  • Pay an entrance fee (membership fee) of Kshs. 1,000.00.

Membership applications

Bunista provides for membership application and variation applications under its prescribed internal procedures.

Items noted: Membership Application, Duties of Members and Variation Applications.

Duties and member rights

Members are not only beneficiaries of services; they are active participants in governance and accountability.

Member duties

  • Attend and participate in decision making at Annual General Meetings and Member Education Days.
  • Comply with policies, by-laws, the Co-operative Society’s Act Cap 490, rules and AGM resolutions.

Member rights and benefits

  • Be elected to organs of the society.
  • Enjoy the use of facilities and services of the society.
