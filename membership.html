Membership

Join a SACCO built around trust, thrift, participation, and real member benefit.

Membership eligibility

  • One must be within the common bond, that is, an employee of JOOUST.
  • Spouse of JOOUST staff.
  • Apply using the prescribed forms.
  • Pay an entrance fee of Kshs. 1,000.00.

Duties of members

  • Attend and participate in decision making at all Annual General Meetings and Member Education Days.
  • Be elected to organs of the society.
  • Enjoy the use of the facilities and services of the society.
  • Comply with policies, By-laws, the Co-operative Society’s Act Cap 490, rules and AGM resolutions.

Membership Application

Prospective members should use the prescribed application forms and submit the required details accurately.
