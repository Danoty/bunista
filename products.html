Credit and Savings Products

Bunista products are designed to support savings growth, emergency needs, development ambitions, asset acquisition, and short-term convenience while maintaining responsible repayment standards.

Loan ProcessingProcessed on Tuesdays and Thursdays on a first come, first served basis.
Appraisal RuleAll loans are processed based on member’s ability to repay under the 1/3 rule.
DisbursementPayments are made through Cheques or M-Pesa for faster access.

Loan facilities

Loans are convenient and easy to access, with transparent terms and no hidden charges. Applications must be duly filled and accompanied by two recent payslips, a copy of ID, and any other necessary attachments as applicable.

Mobile convenience for eligible members

✓
Eligibility

Available to fully registered members who have saved consistently for at least 3 months, with paid share capital of Kshs. 24,000 and minimum deposits of Kshs. 10,000.

✓
Application and repayment

Loan applications and repayment are done through USSD code or the mobile app.

✓
Loan limits

First-time borrowers qualify up to Kshs. 5,000, and can graduate to Kshs. 10,000 based on good repayment history.

✓
30-day facility

Interest is charged at 10%, the loan is unsecured, and members may reapply immediately after full repayment.

Savings Products

Savings channels built for different needs

  • Savings (ordinary shares): Minimum contribution Kshs. 2,000.00, flexible savings, used as loan collateral, with long-term growth value.
  • Christmas Package Scheme: Membership fee Kshs. 1,000.00, minimum contribution Kshs. 1,000.00, 4% annual interest.
  • Education Savings Scheme: Membership fee Kshs. 1,000.00, minimum contribution Kshs. 1,000.00, 4% annual interest.
  • Benevolent and Burial Fund: Monthly contribution Kshs. 500.00 with benefit support for contributor, spouse, children, parents, and in-laws.
