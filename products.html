Credit and Savings Products

All loans are processed based on the member’s ability to repay under the 1/3 rule. Applications are processed on Tuesdays and Thursdays on a first come, first served basis and require completed forms, two recent payslips, copy of ID, and any other necessary attachments as applicable.

Savings Products

  • Savings (ordinary shares): minimum contribution Kshs. 2,000.00, adjustable as needed, used as collateral for loans, and supports long-term saving.
  • Christmas Package Scheme: membership fee Kshs. 1,000.00, minimum contribution Kshs. 1,000.00, and 4% interest after twelve months.
  • Education Savings Scheme: membership fee Kshs. 1,000.00, minimum contribution Kshs. 1,000.00, and 4% interest after twelve months.
  • Benevolent and burial fund: no membership fee, monthly contribution Kshs. 500.00, with benefits for contributor, spouse, children, parents and in-laws.

BunistaMobi Loan Facility

  • Available to fully registered members who have saved consistently for at least 3 months.
  • Fully paid share capital of Kshs. 24,000.00 and minimum deposits of Kshs. 10,000.00 required.
  • Applications and repayment are done via USSD code or mobile app.
  • First-time borrowers qualify for up to Kshs. 5,000.00 and can graduate to Kshs. 10,000.00 based on repayment history.
  • Loan period is 30 days at 10% interest.
  • The loan is unsecured and does not require guarantors.
  • Members may reapply immediately after full repayment.
