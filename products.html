Products & Services

Loans are convenient and easy to get, with processing based on member ability to repay under the 1/3 rule. Payments are done through Cheques or M-Pesa for convenience and faster access.

Credit products

Loan applications are processed on Tuesdays and Thursdays on a first come, first served basis. Applications should be fully filled and accompanied by two recent payslips, a copy of ID, and any other necessary attachments.

ProductKey Details
Normal LoanAfter saving for 6 months, up to 3 times a member’s shares, 9.75% p.a. reducing balance, maximum 48 months.
Super Normal LoanAfter saving for 6 months, up to 4 times a member’s shares, 13% p.a. reducing balance, maximum 48 months.
Emergency LoanAfter saving for 6 months, up to 2 times a member’s shares, 9.75% p.a. reducing balance, maximum 24 months.
Instant LoanAfter saving for 6 months, maximum Kshs. 50,000, interest at 10%, maximum 6 months.
Okoa LoanAfter saving for 6 months, maximum Kshs. 10,000, interest at 10%, repayment in 2 months.
Asset FinancingAfter saving for 6 months, maximum 2x shares, interest at 15%, repayment period 24 months. Includes water tanks, laptops, iPhones, iPads, cookwares, solars and jikos.
Top-up LoanOn normal loans only, qualify after repayment of up to half the outstanding balance, 9.75% p.a., maximum 48 months, premature top-up attracts 2.5% commission on outstanding balance.
Home Development LoanAfter saving for 6 months, up to 3 times a member’s shares, 9.75% p.a. reducing balance, maximum 48 months.
BunistaMobi Loan FacilityShort-term mobile loan for eligible members. First-time borrowers qualify up to Kshs. 5,000 and can graduate up to Kshs. 10,000 based on repayment history.

Savings products

  • Savings (ordinary shares): minimum contribution Kshs. 2,000.00, used as collateral for loans, long-term savings option.
  • Christmas Package Scheme: membership fee Kshs. 1,000.00, minimum contribution Kshs. 1,000.00, twelve-month savings earn 4% interest.
  • Education Savings Scheme: membership fee Kshs. 1,000.00, minimum contribution Kshs. 1,000.00, twelve-month savings earn 4% interest.
  • Benevolent and burial fund: monthly contribution Kshs. 500.00 with defined benefits to contributors and dependants.

BunistaMobi Loan highlights

  • Available to fully registered members with consistent savings for at least 3 months.
  • Requires fully paid share capital of Kshs. 24,000.00 and minimum deposits of Kshs. 10,000.00.
  • Application and repayment via USSD code or mobile app.
  • 30-day repayment period with interest charged at 10%.
  • Unsecured, with no guarantors required.
  • Members may reapply immediately after full repayment.
