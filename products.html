Products and Services

All loans are processed based on member’s ability to repay under the 1/3 rule. Loan applications are processed on Tuesdays and Thursdays on a first come first served basis and should be duly filled with required attachments.

Payments are done through cheques or M-Pesa after appraisal and approval, enabling faster access to funds.

Savings Products

Member savings support long-term growth, credit access and welfare protection.

  • Savings (ordinary shares): Minimum contribution Kshs. 2,000.00; high return long-term savings; savings are used as collateral for loans.
  • Christmas Package Scheme: Membership fee Kshs. 1,000.00; minimum contribution Kshs. 1,000.00; twelve-month savings earn 4% interest.
  • Education Savings Scheme: Membership fee Kshs. 1,000.00; minimum contribution Kshs. 1,000.00; twelve-month savings earn 4% interest.
  • Benevolent and Burial Fund: Monthly contribution Kshs. 500.00 with benefits to contributor, spouse, children, parents and in-laws.

BunistaMobi Loan Facility

A short-term mobile loan facility available to eligible Bunista members.

  • Eligibility: Fully registered members with at least 3 months of consistent savings, fully paid share capital of Kshs. 24,000.00 and minimum deposits of Kshs. 10,000.00.
  • Registration: Members must register for the service before access.
  • Application and repayment: Via USSD code or Mobile App.
  • Loan amount: First-time borrowers up to Kshs. 5,000.00, then gradual increase up to Kshs. 10,000.00 based on repayment history.
  • Period and interest: 30-day repayment period, interest at 10%.
  • Security: Unsecured and does not require guarantors.
  • Repeat loans: Members may reapply immediately after full repayment.
