About Bunista

Bunista Sacco Society Ltd was registered on 24th November 2010 under the Ministry of Co-operative Development and its offices are situated within Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University. Its main objective is to organize and promote the welfare and economic interest of its members.

Welcome to Bunista Sacco Society Ltd

The Sacco draws its membership from Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University employees and their spouses. Since inception, the Sacco has achieved steady growth in membership, share capital, and non-withdrawable deposits while strengthening service delivery to members.

Office base

The Sacco offices are located at the university main campus within the Finance Office Compound behind Procurement Offices, making Bunista accessible to members within the institution.

Objectives

  • To encourage thrift among members.
  • To ensure personal growth through new products and services that promote economic and social welfare.
  • To educate members on proper use of credit, poverty eradication, human dignity, and co-operation.

Universal co-operative principles

  • Voluntary and open membership.
  • Democratic member control.
  • Economic participation by members.
  • Autonomy and independence.
  • Education, training and information.
  • Co-operation among co-operatives.
  • Concern for community in general.
