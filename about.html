About Bunista

Bunista Sacco Society Ltd was registered on 24th November 2010 under the Ministry of Co-operative Development. Its main objective is to organize and promote the welfare and economic interest of its members.

Who we serve

The Sacco draws its membership from Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University employees and their spouses. Since inception, Bunista has experienced steady growth in membership, share capital and non-withdrawable deposits.

Its offices are situated within Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University main campus.

Our objectives

  • To encourage thrift among members.
  • To ensure personal growth through products and services that promote the economic and social base of members.
  • To educate members on proper use of credit, eradicate poverty, preserve human dignity and strengthen co-operation.

Universal co-operative principles

Bunista is guided by proven co-operative values that sustain member trust and participation.
