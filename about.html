History and Profile

The Sacco was registered on 24th November 2010 under the Ministry of Co-operative Development and its offices are situated within Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University. Its main objective is to organize and promote the welfare and economic interest of its members.

Background

Bunista Sacco Society Ltd was registered in November 2010 under the Co-operative Society’s Act Cap 490, Registration Number 12880.

The Sacco draws its membership from Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University employees and their spouses. The offices are located at the university main campus in the Finance Office Compound behind the Procurement Offices.

Since inception, the Sacco has experienced steady growth in membership, share capital and non-withdrawable deposits.

Objectives

The objectives for which the society was established are:

  • To encourage thrift among its members.
  • To ensure personal growth through introduction of new products and services that promote members’ economic and social base.
  • To educate members on proper use of credit, eradicate poverty, uphold human dignity and strengthen co-operation.

Universal co-operative principles

The Sacco is guided by core co-operative values that promote fairness, participation and community impact.

Membership and control

  • Voluntary and open membership
  • Democratic member control
  • Economic participation by members

Freedom and knowledge

  • Autonomy and independence
  • Education, training and information

Solidarity and care

  • Co-operation among co-operatives
  • Concern for community in general
